ADMISSION PROCEDURE

    A candidate seeking admission to school can get the School Brochure cum appling form from the fee counter. Duly filled in form is to be submitted for registration at the fee counter. Incomplete forms shell be rejected.

Document required at the time of admission

  • Transfer Certificate
  • Character Certificate
  • Aadhar Card
  • Photo copy of Class X mark sheet (if no summitted at the time of registration) for candidates seeking admission to std.XI.
